Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

This service ("Meeting SMS") is operated by Alexandria Friends Meeting to send occasional SMS notifications to members and attenders of the Meeting community.

Information we collect

  • Phone numbers — We store the phone numbers of community members who have opted in to receive SMS notifications, as well as the phone numbers of authorized administrators.
  • Message content — We do not store the content of messages after they are sent. Messages are transmitted via Twilio and subject to Twilio's privacy policy.

How we use your information

Phone numbers are used solely to send community notifications (e.g., meeting cancellations, schedule changes) and to authenticate authorized administrators. We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any purpose unrelated to Meeting communications.

Information sharing

We do not sell or share your phone number with third parties. Your number is shared only with Twilio, our SMS delivery provider, for the sole purpose of delivering messages.

Data storage

Phone numbers are stored in a private Google Spreadsheet accessible only to Meeting clerks. The spreadsheet is not publicly accessible.

Opting out

You can stop receiving messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. To opt back in, reply START. You may also ask any Meeting clerk to remove your number from the list.

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact the clerks of Alexandria Friends Meeting.
