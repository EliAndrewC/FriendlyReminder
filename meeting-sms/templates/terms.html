Terms of Service

Last updated: March 22, 2026

This service ("Meeting SMS") is operated by Alexandria Friends Meeting to send SMS notifications to its community.

Use of the service

This service is available to members and attenders of Alexandria Friends Meeting. Authorized administrators may use this service to send community notifications such as meeting cancellations, schedule changes, and other time-sensitive announcements.

Message frequency and content

Messages are sent infrequently and only when there is a need to communicate time-sensitive information to the Meeting community. Message frequency varies but is typically no more than a few messages per month.

Message and data rates

Standard message and data rates from your wireless carrier may apply.

Opting out

You may opt out of receiving messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance. You may also contact a Meeting clerk to be removed from the list.

Disclaimer

This service is provided on a volunteer basis by the clerks of Alexandria Friends Meeting. We make no guarantees about message delivery or service availability.

Contact

For questions about these terms, contact the clerks of Alexandria Friends Meeting.
